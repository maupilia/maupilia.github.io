Going further

Using SARDARA (the ROACH2-boards backend) instead of the DFB will bring some obvious advantages:

- high timing resolution;
- high frequency resolution;
- coherent dedispersion.

At the moment the pulsar-mode is not operative in SARDARA due to I/O issues with the writing speed and data volume.

Figure 9. ROACH 2 boards: they constitute the new SArdinia Roach2-based Architecture for Radio Astronomy (SARDARA) backend. Pulsar mode foreseen by the beginning of 2017. (Credits SRT Tender Team).
